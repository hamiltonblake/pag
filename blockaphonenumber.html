Block a phone number

In this guide you will learn how to block a phone number on an iPhone or Android phone. 

iPhone Android

Block a phone number on your iPhone

  1. Open the Phone app. You will find the icon on your Home Screen or in the springboard at the bottom of your screen.
  2.  Tap Recents or Contacts. You want to block a number that is not in your contacts and called you recently ? Then you have to tap Recents, while you will tap Contacts to block a contact in your phone.
  3. Tap the “i” in a circle. In the top right of the number you want to block. If you want to block a contact instead, just tap the name of the contact.
  4. Tap Block this Caller. Scroll down and tap on Block this Caller

Block a phone number on your Android phone

  1. Open the Phone app. You will find the app on the main screen of your phone.
  2. Tap on ⋮  You can find it in the top-right corner of your Phone screen. After the tap a drop-down menu will show up.
  3. Now tap on Settings
  4. Tap on Call Blocking. It is near the top of the page.
  5. Tap "ADD A NUMBER". Shows up at the top of the page.
  6. Enter the number you want to block. Then tap in the text field and enter the number you want to block.
